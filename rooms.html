THE NIGHTMARE

When you wake up to find you're not alone...
This is a haunted house, escape room, immersive theater hybrid. 

Time Limit

Experienced escape room participants can expect this to be a 10 minute room. Participants have a 20 minute time limit to escape.  

Participants

Participants must be age 14 or older. All minors (17 & younger) need a parent or guardian to sign a release before participating in The Nightmare: Escape Haunt. The room is built for groups of 4.
